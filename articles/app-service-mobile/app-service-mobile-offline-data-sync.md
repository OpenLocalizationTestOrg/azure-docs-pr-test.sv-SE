## <span data-ttu-id="bbffc-143"><a name="how-sync-works"></a>Hur offline synkronisering fungerar</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bbffc-143"><a name="how-sync-works"></a>How offline synchronization works</span></span>
